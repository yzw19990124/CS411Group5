Spotify Playlist for Your Mood!

Do you ever not know what to listen to? Do you want a playlist generated for your exact mood? Look no further!

By simply uploading an image of your face, you can get a Spotify playlist aligned with your exact mood.

**How does this work?**

This application uses facial recognition software to analyse the inputted image and rates its emotion on a scale. Then this emotion is used with Spotify's database to construct the ideal playlist.
